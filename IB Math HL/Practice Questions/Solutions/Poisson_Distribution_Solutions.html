Q1EASYPaper 1 (no calc)Poisson PMF[5 marks]

$X \sim \text{Po}(2)$, $e^{-2} = 0.1353$. Find $P(X = 0)$, $P(X = 1)$, $P(X = 2)$.$X \sim \text{Po}(2)$，$e^{-2} = 0.1353$。求 $P(X = 0)$、$P(X = 1)$、$P(X = 2)$。

Answers:答案：  (a) $0.1353$  ·  (b) $0.2707$  ·  (c) $0.2707$ (equal to (b))（与 (b) 相等）

(a) A1

$P(X = 0) = \dfrac{e^{-2} 2^{0}}{0!} = e^{-2} = \boxed{0.1353}.$

(b) M1·A1

$P(X = 1) = \dfrac{e^{-2} 2^{1}}{1!} = 2e^{-2} = 2(0.1353) = \boxed{0.2707}.$

(c) M1·A1

$P(X = 2) = \dfrac{e^{-2} 2^{2}}{2!} = \dfrac{4}{2}e^{-2} = 2e^{-2} = \boxed{0.2707}.$ This is equal to $P(X = 1)$.
Why (b) = (c). Consecutive Poisson probabilities satisfy $\dfrac{P(X = k+1)}{P(X = k)} = \dfrac{\lambda}{k+1}$. With $\lambda = 2$, the step from $k = 1$ to $k = 2$ multiplies by $\tfrac{2}{2} = 1$, so $P(X = 1) = P(X = 2)$. The distribution has two modes, at $1$ and $2$. In general, when $\lambda$ is a positive integer the modes are at $\lambda - 1$ and $\lambda$.

(a) A1

$P(X = 0) = \dfrac{e^{-2} 2^{0}}{0!} = e^{-2} = \boxed{0.1353}.$

(b) M1·A1

$P(X = 1) = \dfrac{e^{-2} 2^{1}}{1!} = 2e^{-2} = 2(0.1353) = \boxed{0.2707}.$

(c) M1·A1

$P(X = 2) = \dfrac{e^{-2} 2^{2}}{2!} = \dfrac{4}{2}e^{-2} = 2e^{-2} = \boxed{0.2707}.$ 该值与 $P(X = 1)$ 相等。

Q2EASYPaper 1 (no calc)Mean & Variance[4 marks]

$X \sim \text{Po}(3.5)$. State $E(X)$, $\text{Var}(X)$, the standard deviation, and the defining feature.$X \sim \text{Po}(3.5)$。写出 $E(X)$、$\text{Var}(X)$、标准差，以及其定义性特征。

Answers:答案：  (a) $E(X) = \text{Var}(X) = 3.5$  ·  (b) $\sigma \approx 1.871$  ·  (c) mean = variance均值 = 方差

(a) A1·A1

For $X \sim \text{Po}(\lambda)$, $E(X) = \lambda$ and $\text{Var}(X) = \lambda$, so $E(X) = \boxed{3.5}$ and $\text{Var}(X) = \boxed{3.5}$.

(b) A1

$\sigma = \sqrt{\text{Var}(X)} = \sqrt{3.5} \approx \boxed{1.871}.$

(c) R1

The Poisson distribution has its mean equal to its variance ($E(X) = \text{Var}(X) = \lambda$).
A diagnostic for real data. If a data set of counts has sample variance noticeably larger than its sample mean, it is overdispersed and Poisson is a poor model (a negative-binomial often fits better); variance much smaller signals underdispersion. Checking $\text{variance} \approx \text{mean}$ is the quickest sanity test for "is this Poisson?"

(a) A1·A1

对 $X \sim \text{Po}(\lambda)$，有 $E(X) = \lambda$ 且 $\text{Var}(X) = \lambda$，故 $E(X) = \boxed{3.5}$，$\text{Var}(X) = \boxed{3.5}$。

(b) A1

$\sigma = \sqrt{\text{Var}(X)} = \sqrt{3.5} \approx \boxed{1.871}.$

(c) R1

泊松分布的均值等于方差（$E(X) = \text{Var}(X) = \lambda$）。
判断真实数据的快速准则。若一组计数数据的样本方差明显大于样本均值，则称为过离散（overdispersion），泊松模型不适用（常用负二项分布更佳）；方差远小于均值则为欠离散。检查"方差 $\approx$ 均值"是判断"是否服从泊松"最快的检验。
Q3MEDIUMPaper 1 (no calc)Cumulative Probability[6 marks]

Help-desk calls $\sim \text{Po}(1.5)$ per minute, $e^{-1.5} = 0.2231$. Find $P(0)$, $P(\ge 1)$, $P(\le 2)$.求助热线来电 $\sim \text{Po}(1.5)$（每分钟），$e^{-1.5} = 0.2231$。求 $P(0)$、$P(\ge 1)$、$P(\le 2)$。

Answers:答案：  (a) $0.2231$  ·  (b) $0.7769$  ·  (c) $0.8088$

(a) A1

$P(X = 0) = e^{-1.5} = \boxed{0.2231}.$

(b) M1·A1

$P(X \ge 1) = 1 - P(X = 0) = 1 - 0.2231 = \boxed{0.7769}.$

(c) M1·A1·A1

$$ P(X \le 2) = e^{-1.5}\!\left(1 + 1.5 + \frac{1.5^{2}}{2}\right) = 0.2231\,(1 + 1.5 + 1.125) = 0.2231 \times 3.625 = \boxed{0.8088}. $$
"At least one" almost always means complement. $P(X \ge 1) = 1 - P(X = 0)$ is the single most common Poisson exam step. And note $P(X \le 2)$ needs the three terms $k = 0, 1, 2$ summed — a frequent slip is to forget the $k = 0$ term.

(a) A1

$P(X = 0) = e^{-1.5} = \boxed{0.2231}.$

(b) M1·A1

$P(X \ge 1) = 1 - P(X = 0) = 1 - 0.2231 = \boxed{0.7769}.$

(c) M1·A1·A1

$$ P(X \le 2) = e^{-1.5}\!\left(1 + 1.5 + \frac{1.5^{2}}{2}\right) = 0.2231\,(1 + 1.5 + 1.125) = 0.2231 \times 3.625 = \boxed{0.8088}. $$
"至少一次"几乎总是用补事件。$P(X \ge 1) = 1 - P(X = 0)$ 是泊松题最常用的一步。另外 $P(X \le 2)$ 需要把 $k = 0, 1, 2$ 三项相加 —— 常见失误是漏掉 $k = 0$ 这一项。

Q4MEDIUMPaper 2 (calc)Poisson cdf[7 marks]

$X \sim \text{Po}(8)$ emails per hour. Find $P(X = 10)$, $P(X \le 5)$, $P(6 \le X \le 10)$.$X \sim \text{Po}(8)$（每小时邮件数）。求 $P(X = 10)$、$P(X \le 5)$、$P(6 \le X \le 10)$。

Answers:答案：  (a) $0.0993$  ·  (b) $0.191$  ·  (c) $0.625$

(a) M1·A1

poissonpdf(8, 10): $P(X = 10) = \dfrac{e^{-8} 8^{10}}{10!} = \boxed{0.0993}$ (3 s.f.).

(b) M1·A1

poissoncdf(8, 5): $P(X \le 5) = \boxed{0.191}$ (3 s.f.).

(c) M1·M1·A1

$$ P(6 \le X \le 10) = P(X \le 10) - P(X \le 5) = 0.8159 - 0.1912 = \boxed{0.625}. $$ On the GDC: poissoncdf(8, 10) − poissoncdf(8, 5).
Mind the endpoints. $P(6 \le X \le 10)$ is inclusive of both ends, so subtract $P(X \le 5)$ (not $P(X \le 6)$). The reliable template for any inclusive range is $P(a \le X \le b) = \text{cdf}(b) - \text{cdf}(a - 1)$ for integer $a$.

(a) M1·A1

poissonpdf(8, 10)：$P(X = 10) = \dfrac{e^{-8} 8^{10}}{10!} = \boxed{0.0993}$（3 位有效数字）。

(b) M1·A1

poissoncdf(8, 5)：$P(X \le 5) = \boxed{0.191}$（3 位有效数字）。

(c) M1·M1·A1

$$ P(6 \le X \le 10) = P(X \le 10) - P(X \le 5) = 0.8159 - 0.1912 = \boxed{0.625}. $$ 在 GDC 上：poissoncdf(8, 10) − poissoncdf(8, 5)。
注意端点。$P(6 \le X \le 10)$ 两端都含，因此要减去 $P(X \le 5)$（而非 $P(X \le 6)$）。对整数 $a$，含端点区间的可靠模板为 $P(a \le X \le b) = \text{cdf}(b) - \text{cdf}(a - 1)$。
Q5MEDIUMPaper 2 (calc)Scaling the Interval[6 marks]

Flaws at $2.5$ per metre (Poisson). Distribution over $4$ m; $P(\text{exactly } 8)$ in $4$ m; $P(\ge 1)$ in $0.5$ m.瑕疵每米 $2.5$ 个（泊松）。求 $4$ m 上的分布、$4$ m 上恰 $8$ 个的概率、$0.5$ m 上至少一个的概率。

Answers:答案：  (a) $\text{Po}(10)$  ·  (b) $0.1126$  ·  (c) $0.713$

(a) Scale the rate A1

Over $4$ m the mean is $2.5 \times 4 = 10$, so the number of flaws $\sim \boxed{\text{Po}(10)}.$

(b) M1·A1

poissonpdf(10, 8): $P = \dfrac{e^{-10} 10^{8}}{8!} = \boxed{0.1126}$ (3 s.f.).

(c) M1·M1·A1

Over $0.5$ m the mean is $2.5 \times 0.5 = 1.25$, so flaws $\sim \text{Po}(1.25)$, and $$ P(X \ge 1) = 1 - e^{-1.25} = 1 - 0.2865 = \boxed{0.713}. $$
The rate is per unit; rescale before computing. A Poisson process over an interval of length $t$ with rate $\lambda$ per unit has parameter $\lambda t$. Every "different length / different time window" sub-question is just a new $\lambda t$ — set it first, then use pdf/cdf.

(a) 对速率进行缩放 A1

$4$ m 上均值为 $2.5 \times 4 = 10$，故瑕疵数 $\sim \boxed{\text{Po}(10)}.$

(b) M1·A1

poissonpdf(10, 8)：$P = \dfrac{e^{-10} 10^{8}}{8!} = \boxed{0.1126}$（3 位有效数字）。

(c) M1·M1·A1

$0.5$ m 上均值为 $2.5 \times 0.5 = 1.25$，故瑕疵数 $\sim \text{Po}(1.25)$，于是 $$ P(X \ge 1) = 1 - e^{-1.25} = 1 - 0.2865 = \boxed{0.713}. $$
速率是"每单位"的，计算前先按比例换算。速率为每单位 $\lambda$ 的泊松过程，在长度为 $t$ 的区间上参数为 $\lambda t$。凡是"不同长度／不同时间窗"的小题，都只是一个新的 $\lambda t$ —— 先定出它，再用 pdf/cdf。

Q6HARDPaper 2 (calc)Sum of Independent Poissons[10 marks]

$X \sim \text{Po}(3)$ sales, $Y \sim \text{Po}(5)$ support, independent. $T = X + Y$. Find the distribution of $T$, $P(T = 10)$, $P(T \ge 6)$, and $P(X = 3 \mid T = 8)$.$X \sim \text{Po}(3)$（销售），$Y \sim \text{Po}(5)$（支持），相互独立。$T = X + Y$。求 $T$ 的分布、$P(T = 10)$、$P(T \ge 6)$ 与 $P(X = 3 \mid T = 8)$。

Answers:答案：  (a) $T \sim \text{Po}(8)$  ·  (b) $0.0993$  ·  (c) $0.809$  ·  (d) $0.282$

(a) Independent Poissons add A1·A1

$T = X + Y \sim \text{Po}(3 + 5) = \boxed{\text{Po}(8)}.$ (Sum of independent Poissons is Poisson with the parameters added.)

(b) M1·A1

poissonpdf(8, 10): $P(T = 10) = \boxed{0.0993}$ (3 s.f.).

(c) M1·A1

$$ P(T \ge 6) = 1 - P(T \le 5) = 1 - 0.1912 = \boxed{0.809}. $$

(d) Conditional split M1·M1·A1·A1

Given the total $T = 8$, each enquiry is independently a sales call with probability $\dfrac{\lambda_X}{\lambda_X + \lambda_Y} = \dfrac{3}{8}$, so $X \mid (T = 8) \sim B\!\left(8, \tfrac{3}{8}\right)$. Then $$ P(X = 3 \mid T = 8) = \binom{8}{3}\!\left(\tfrac{3}{8}\right)^{3}\!\left(\tfrac{5}{8}\right)^{5} = 56 \cdot \tfrac{27}{512} \cdot \tfrac{3125}{32768} = \boxed{0.282}\ (3\text{ s.f.}). $$
The conditional-split theorem. If $X \sim \text{Po}(\lambda_X)$ and $Y \sim \text{Po}(\lambda_Y)$ are independent, then conditional on $X + Y = n$, $\;X \sim B\!\left(n, \tfrac{\lambda_X}{\lambda_X + \lambda_Y}\right)$. The overall rate cancels — only the proportion $3{:}5$ survives. This "Poisson thinning" idea is what lets you split one Poisson stream into independent sub-streams (e.g. arrivals routed by type).

(a) 独立泊松相加 A1·A1

$T = X + Y \sim \text{Po}(3 + 5) = \boxed{\text{Po}(8)}.$（独立泊松之和仍为泊松，参数相加。）

(b) M1·A1

poissonpdf(8, 10)：$P(T = 10) = \boxed{0.0993}$（3 位有效数字）。

(c) M1·A1

$$ P(T \ge 6) = 1 - P(T \le 5) = 1 - 0.1912 = \boxed{0.809}. $$

(d) 条件分裂 M1·M1·A1·A1

已知总数 $T = 8$ 时，每次咨询独立地以概率 $\dfrac{\lambda_X}{\lambda_X + \lambda_Y} = \dfrac{3}{8}$ 为销售咨询，故 $X \mid (T = 8) \sim B\!\left(8, \tfrac{3}{8}\right)$。于是 $$ P(X = 3 \mid T = 8) = \binom{8}{3}\!\left(\tfrac{3}{8}\right)^{3}\!\left(\tfrac{5}{8}\right)^{5} = 56 \cdot \tfrac{27}{512} \cdot \tfrac{3125}{32768} = \boxed{0.282}\ (3\text{ 位有效数字}). $$
条件分裂定理。若 $X \sim \text{Po}(\lambda_X)$ 与 $Y \sim \text{Po}(\lambda_Y)$ 独立，则在 $X + Y = n$ 的条件下，$\;X \sim B\!\left(n, \tfrac{\lambda_X}{\lambda_X + \lambda_Y}\right)$。总速率被约去，只留下比例 $3{:}5$。这一"泊松稀释"（thinning）思想正是把一条泊松到达流按类型拆分为多条独立子流的依据。
Q7HARDPaper 2 (calc)Poisson Approximation to Binomial[8 marks]

Defective with $p = 0.005$, batch $n = 400$, $D = $ number defective. State exact distribution; justify Poisson approx; find $P(D = 0)$, $P(D \ge 3)$ by approximation; then exact $P(D = 0)$ and compare.次品概率 $p = 0.005$，每批 $n = 400$，$D = $ 次品数。写出精确分布；说明泊松近似的合理性；用近似求 $P(D = 0)$、$P(D \ge 3)$；再求精确 $P(D = 0)$ 并比较。

Answers:答案：  (a) $B(400,\, 0.005)$  ·  (b) $D \approx \text{Po}(2)$  ·  (c) $0.1353,\ 0.323$  ·  (d) $0.1346$ (very close)（非常接近）

(a) A1

$D \sim \boxed{B(400,\, 0.005)}$ — a fixed number of independent trials, each defective with constant probability.

(b) Justify Poisson R1·A1

$n$ is large and $p$ is small (with $np$ moderate), so the binomial is well approximated by a Poisson with $$ \lambda = np = 400 \times 0.005 = 2, \qquad D \approx \text{Po}(2). $$

(c) M1·A1·A1

$P(D = 0) = e^{-2} = \boxed{0.1353}.$ $$ P(D \ge 3) = 1 - P(D \le 2) = 1 - e^{-2}\!\left(1 + 2 + \tfrac{2^{2}}{2}\right) = 1 - 5e^{-2} = 1 - 0.6767 = \boxed{0.323}. $$

(d) Exact binomial and comparison M1·A1

$$ P(D = 0) = (1 - 0.005)^{400} = 0.995^{400} = \boxed{0.1346}. $$ This differs from the Poisson value $0.1353$ by only about $0.0007$, confirming the approximation is excellent.
Poisson is the rare-event limit of the binomial. As $n \to \infty$ and $p \to 0$ with $np = \lambda$ held fixed, $B(n, p) \to \text{Po}(\lambda)$. Rule of thumb for using it: $n \ge 50$ and $p \le 0.1$ (so $np \lesssim 5$). The pay-off is that one parameter $\lambda$ replaces two ($n, p$), and the awkward $\binom{n}{k}$ with huge $n$ becomes a clean $e^{-\lambda}\lambda^{k}/k!$.

(a) A1

$D \sim \boxed{B(400,\, 0.005)}$ —— 固定次数的独立试验，每次以恒定概率为次品。

(b) 说明泊松近似 R1·A1

$n$ 大、$p$ 小（且 $np$ 适中），故二项分布可由泊松分布很好地近似，参数为 $$ \lambda = np = 400 \times 0.005 = 2, \qquad D \approx \text{Po}(2). $$

(c) M1·A1·A1

$P(D = 0) = e^{-2} = \boxed{0.1353}.$ $$ P(D \ge 3) = 1 - P(D \le 2) = 1 - e^{-2}\!\left(1 + 2 + \tfrac{2^{2}}{2}\right) = 1 - 5e^{-2} = 1 - 0.6767 = \boxed{0.323}. $$

(d) 精确二项值与比较 M1·A1

$$ P(D = 0) = (1 - 0.005)^{400} = 0.995^{400} = \boxed{0.1346}. $$ 与泊松近似值 $0.1353$ 仅相差约 $0.0007$，说明近似效果极佳。
泊松是二项分布的稀有事件极限。当 $n \to \infty$、$p \to 0$ 且保持 $np = \lambda$ 不变时，$B(n, p) \to \text{Po}(\lambda)$。使用准则：$n \ge 50$ 且 $p \le 0.1$（即 $np \lesssim 5$）。好处是用一个参数 $\lambda$ 取代两个（$n, p$），并把 $n$ 极大时难算的 $\binom{n}{k}$ 化为简洁的 $e^{-\lambda}\lambda^{k}/k!$。
