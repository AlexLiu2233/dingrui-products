FRQ 1MEDIUM 2.2 / 2.4 Atwood with Table FrictionCalculator

A light, inextensible string runs from $m_1$ over a frictionless, massless pulley at the edge of the table to a hanging block $m_2 = 4.0~\mathrm{kg}$. The system is released from rest.
