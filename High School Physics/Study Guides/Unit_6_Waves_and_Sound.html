Waves and Sound波与声

A wave is a travelling disturbance that carries energy without carrying matter. This guide builds the whole topic from the ground up: the split between transverse and longitudinal waves and the anatomy (wavelength, frequency, period, amplitude) that describes any of them; the single universal wave equation $v = f\lambda$ that ties speed, frequency, and wavelength together; the principle of superposition and the constructive and destructive interference it produces; standing waves and resonance on strings and in air columns, where the harmonic series $f_n = \tfrac{nv}{2L}$ first appears; sound as a longitudinal pressure wave and how its intensity falls off; the Doppler effect that shifts the pitch of a passing siren; and finally beats, the slow throb of two near-equal frequencies. Worked examples use real numbers throughout.波（wave，波）是携带能量却不携带物质的传播扰动。本指南从头建立整个主题：横波与纵波之分，以及描述任何波的构造（波长、频率、周期、振幅）；把速度、频率、波长系于一体的唯一通用波动方程 $v = f\lambda$；叠加原理及其产生的相长与相消干涉（interference，干涉）；弦上与空气柱中的驻波（standing wave，驻波）与共振（resonance，共振），谐波列 $f_n = \tfrac{nv}{2L}$ 在此首次出现；作为纵向压力波的声音（sound wave，声波）及其声强如何衰减；使经过的警笛音高偏移的多普勒效应（Doppler effect，多普勒效应）；以及最后的拍（beats，拍），即两个近乎相等频率的缓慢颤动。全部例题均用真实数字演算。

How to use this guide如何使用本指南

Waves and Sound appears as a standalone unit in three of the four curricula we map to, and they agree almost completely on the core: wave types, the anatomy of a wave, the universal equation $v = f\lambda$, superposition and interference, standing waves and resonance, and the basics of sound. They diverge on the quantitative Doppler effect. US NGSS assessment is explicitly "limited to algebraic relationships and describing those relationships qualitatively," so the quantitative Doppler work in §6 sits above the NGSS-assessed floor. Alberta Physics 20 (outcome 20–D2.9k) demands the Doppler effect "qualitatively and quantitatively," and BC Physics 11 lists "Doppler shift" as a wave behaviour. The table tells you which sections are core for you right now; each row cites the curriculum document it was checked against.在我们对照的四套大纲中，《波与声》在其中三套里作为独立单元出现，且它们在核心上几乎完全一致：波的类型、波的构造、通用方程 $v = f\lambda$、叠加与干涉、驻波与共振，以及声音的基础。它们的分歧在于定量多普勒效应。US NGSS 的评估明确"限于代数关系及对这些关系的定性描述"，因此 §6 的定量多普勒内容高于 NGSS 被评估的下限。阿尔伯塔 Physics 20（outcome 20–D2.9k）要求"定性与定量地"处理多普勒效应；BC Physics 11 把"多普勒频移"列为一种波的行为。下表告诉你当前哪些节属于你的核心；每行都注明所依据的课纲文件。

If you are cramming the night before如果你在临阵磨枪

Memorise four things: the universal wave equation $v = f\lambda$ and that $f = 1/T$; the transverse/longitudinal split (string wave vs sound); that interference is just superposition (in-phase adds, out-of-phase cancels); and the harmonic series for a string or open pipe, $f_n = \tfrac{nv}{2L}$. Read every cram-cheat box. Skip the going-deeper derivations and treat the Doppler effect qualitatively (pitch rises on approach, falls on recession).背熟四件事：通用波动方程 $v = f\lambda$ 以及 $f = 1/T$；横波／纵波之分（弦上波与声音）；干涉不过是叠加（同相相加，反相相消）；以及弦或开管的谐波列 $f_n = \tfrac{nv}{2L}$。读每个速记框。跳过深入推导，对多普勒效应作定性处理（靠近时音高升高，远离时降低）。

*
If you are going for the top mark如果你目标顶分

Keep your units consistent: convert $\mathrm{cm}$ to $\mathrm{m}$ and keep frequency in $\mathrm{Hz}$ before applying $v=f\lambda$. Know which end of a string or pipe is a node and which is an antinode before writing $f_n$. For standing waves, sketch the harmonic and count half-wavelengths rather than memorising every case. Alberta Physics 20 (20–D2.9k) expects you to compute a Doppler-shifted frequency, not just state the direction of the shift — practise both source-approaching and source-receding signs.保持单位一致：应用 $v=f\lambda$ 前把 $\mathrm{cm}$ 换成 $\mathrm{m}$，频率保持 $\mathrm{Hz}$。写 $f_n$ 前先弄清弦或管的哪端是波节、哪端是波腹。处理驻波时，画出该谐波并数半波长，而非死记每种情形。阿尔伯塔 Physics 20（20–D2.9k）要求你计算多普勒频移后的频率，而非仅说明频移方向——声源靠近与远离两种符号都要练。

Honors flag.荣誉级标记。 Section 6 (The Doppler Effect) carries the Honors chip for the US NGSS track, where the Assessment Boundary for HS-PS4-1 is "limited to algebraic relationships and describing those relationships qualitatively." It is core, not honors, in Alberta Physics 20 (outcome 20–D2.9k requires the Doppler effect "qualitatively and quantitatively") and BC Physics 11 (Content lists "Doppler shift"). If your row above sends you to §6 for quantitative work, treat it as required content, not enrichment.§6（多普勒效应）在 US NGSS 轨道上标 Honors，因为 HS-PS4-1 的评估边界"限于代数关系及对这些关系的定性描述"。但在阿尔伯塔 Physics 20（outcome 20–D2.9k 要求"定性与定量地"处理多普勒效应）与 BC Physics 11（内容含"多普勒频移"）中，它是核心而非荣誉内容。如果你的行把你引向 §6 的定量内容，就把它视为必学，不是拓展。
