# Gilbert Strang, Calculus (MIT, free) — chapter list (content source)

Free textbook published by MIT; CC-licensed. Strang's prose is excellent for intuition and the "why" that the
mastery-reader half of the dual-goal contract needs. URLs:
- OCW landing: https://ocw.mit.edu/resources/res-18-001-calculus-online-textbook-spring-2005/
- Author page: https://math.mit.edu/~gs/

Chapters:
1. Introduction to Calculus (velocity and distance, slope of a curve, the derivative and integral as a pair)
2. Derivatives (limits, derivative of a function, slope and rate of change, limits and continuity)
3. Applications of the Derivative (max/min, second derivative, graphs, linear approximation, Newton's method)
4. The Chain Rule (derivatives by the chain rule, implicit differentiation and related rates, inverse functions,
   inverses of trig functions)
5. Integrals (antiderivatives, definite integral, Fundamental Theorem, properties, numerical integration)
6. Exponentials and Logarithms (e^x, derivatives, growth and decay, separable equations, hyperbolic functions)
7. Techniques of Integration (by parts, trigonometric integrals, partial fractions, improper integrals)
8. Applications of the Integral (area, volume, length, work, average value, probability, mass and moments)
9. Polar Coordinates and Complex Numbers
10. Infinite Series (the geometric series, convergence tests, Taylor series, power series)
11. Vectors and Matrices (vectors, dot and cross products, planes, linear algebra basics)
12. Motion Along a Curve (vector-valued functions, velocity and acceleration, curvature)
13. Partial Derivatives (surfaces, partials, tangent planes and linear approximation, gradient, chain rule,
    maxima/minima and saddle points, Lagrange multipliers)
14. Multiple Integrals (double integrals, polar/cylindrical/spherical, change of variables)
15. Vector Calculus (vector fields, line integrals, Green's theorem, surface integrals, divergence theorem,
    Stokes' theorem)
16. Mathematics after Calculus (linear algebra, differential equations preview)

Grounds: A1-A8 (Ch 1-5), B1-B8 (Ch 7-10), C1-C8 (Ch 11-15). ODE material is light in Strang (Ch 6 separable,
Ch 16 preview); for group D defer to 18.03 / Paul's Online Notes.
