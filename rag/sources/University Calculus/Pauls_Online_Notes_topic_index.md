# Paul's Online Notes — Calc I/II/III + Differential Equations (content source)

Free notes by Paul Dawkins (Lamar University). Clean, exhaustive worked-example style; the single best free
ground for METHOD and step structure, especially for group D (ODEs). URL: https://tutorial.math.lamar.edu/

## Calculus I -> group A (+ start of B)
- Review (functions, trig, exp/log, solving equations)
- Limits (tangent/rate problems, one-sided limits, limit properties, computing limits, infinite limits, limits
  at infinity, continuity, the precise definition of a limit)
- Derivatives (definition, interpretation, differentiation formulas, product/quotient rule, derivatives of trig,
  exp/log, inverse trig, hyperbolic, chain rule, implicit differentiation, related rates, higher-order)
- Applications of Derivatives (rates of change, critical points, min/max, the shape of a graph, Mean Value
  Theorem, optimization, L'Hospital's rule, linear approximation, differentials, Newton's method)
- Integrals (indefinite, computing indefinite, substitution, definite, FTC, substitution for definite)
- Applications of Integrals (average function value, area between curves, volumes of revolution by rings/cylinders, work)
Grounds: A1 (Limits), A2-A3 (Derivatives), A4 (Derivatives), A5 (related rates/implicit), A6 (L'Hospital/linear
approx), A7 (applications), A8 (Integrals).

## Calculus II -> group B
- Integration Techniques (by parts, trig integrals, trig substitution, partial fractions, improper integrals,
  approximating definite integrals)
- Applications of Integrals (arc length, surface area, center of mass, hydrostatic pressure, probability)
- Parametric Equations and Polar Coordinates (parametric, tangents/area/arc length with parametric, polar
  coordinates, area/arc length with polar)
- Series and Sequences (sequences, series basics, integral/comparison/alternating/ratio/root tests, power series,
  Taylor series, binomial series)
Grounds: B1-B2 (Integration Techniques), B3 (improper/approximating), B4-B5 (Applications), B6-B7 (Series), B8 (Parametric/Polar).

## Calculus III -> group C
- Three Dimensional Space (vectors, dot/cross product, lines, planes, quadric surfaces, vector functions,
  calculus with vector functions, tangent/normal vectors, arc length and curvature, velocity/acceleration,
  cylindrical/spherical coordinates)
- Partial Derivatives (limits, partial derivatives, interpretations, higher-order, differentials, chain rule,
  directional derivatives and the gradient)
- Applications of Partial Derivatives (tangent planes/linear approximation, gradient, relative and absolute
  extrema, Lagrange multipliers)
- Multiple Integrals (double integrals, double integrals in polar, triple integrals, cylindrical/spherical,
  change of variables, surface area)
- Line Integrals (vector fields, line integrals, fundamental theorem, conservative vector fields, Green's theorem)
- Surface Integrals (parametric surfaces, surface integrals, curl and divergence, Stokes' theorem, divergence theorem)
Grounds: C1-C2 (3D space + vector functions), C3 (Partial Derivatives), C4-C5 (Applications), C6 (Multiple
Integrals), C7 (Line Integrals/Green's), C8 (Surface Integrals/Stokes'/Divergence).

## Differential Equations -> group D
- Basic Concepts (definitions, direction fields, final thoughts)
- First Order DE (linear, separable, exact, Bernoulli, substitutions, intervals of validity, modeling,
  equilibrium solutions, Euler's method)
- Second Order DE (basic concepts, real/complex/repeated roots, reduction of order, undetermined coefficients,
  variation of parameters, mechanical vibrations)
- Laplace Transforms (definition, transforms, inverse transforms, solving IVPs, step functions, Dirac delta,
  convolution)
- Systems of DE (matrix review, eigenvalues/eigenvectors, phase plane, real/complex/repeated eigenvalues)
- Series Solutions (review power series, series solutions, Euler equations)
- Higher Order DE; Boundary Value Problems and Fourier Series; Partial DE (beyond group D scope)
Grounds: D1 (First Order linear/separable), D2 (exact/modeling), D3 (Second Order homogeneous roots), D4
(undetermined coeffs/variation of parameters), D5 (mechanical vibrations), D6 (Laplace), D7 (Systems), D8 (Series
solutions + Euler's method/numerical).
